Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) focuses on identifying negative thought patterns and replacing them with constructive ones.

Grounding exercise: Name 5 things you see, 4 things you feel, 3 things you hear, 2 things you smell, 1 thing you taste.

Inspirational Quote: "Everybody has a plan until they get punched in the mouth" — Mike Tyson

Journaling prompt: Write down 3 positive things that happened today, no matter how small.

Anxiety Tip: Take slow, deep breaths — inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 6.
